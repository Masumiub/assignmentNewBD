Bangladesh is preparing to launch its second satellite

Date: 07/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh is preparing to launch its second satellite, Shahjahan Mahmood, in the next two to three years. The satellite will be used for monitoring natural disasters, assessing environmental changes, and managing agricultural resources.

BAF's first homemade fighter

Date: 05/08/2025 Time: 07.25 PM

The BBT-1 is the first aircraft built by the Bangladesh Air Force (BAF) using their own technology. It is the prototype of the Bangabandhu Basic Trainer (BBT) aircraft, which is a military basic trainer and light attack aircraft. The BBT-1 has the following features: Flying capacity: 5.5-6 hours, Fuel consumption: 12-16 liters per hour.

Bangladesh's first drone

Date: 09/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh's first drone is the Northbird 0.1, an unmanned air system (UAS) developed by DhumketuX. The Northbird is designed for military missions like intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR), but can also be used for civil applications. DhumketuX also developed their own control station and communications systems for the Northbird.
